Ben Steel

Ben Steel is dedicated to improving lives across the country through policy and action.

Biography

Ben Steel moved around a lot while he was a child, living in the North, the South, and the West Country. He studied engineering and environmental studies in college, eventually earning a master's degree in policy studies. Since 2016, Ben has served in the House of Representatives, leading numerous committees and advancing key policies. Currently, Ben is 55 years old, living with his wife, his mother, and his son, who is currently in college.
